Acceptance of Your Order

Orders placed through CoffeeHouse are subject to acceptance and availability. We reserve the right to reject or cancel any order under certain circumstances.

  1. Products are out of stock.
  2. Payment authorization fails.
  3. Incorrect pricing information is displayed.

  6. Eligibility requirements are not met.
