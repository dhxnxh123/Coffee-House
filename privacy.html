PRIVACY POLICY

Our Privacy Promise

Your privacy is our utmost priority. Any personal information you provide to CoffeeHouse will be used only for delivering products and services, processing orders, maintaining your account, and improving your experience with our website.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring that your data is handled responsibly and securely.

How Do We Use Your Information?

  1. Process your orders and communicate with you regarding purchases.
  2. Verify payments and prevent fraudulent transactions.
  3. Maintain and manage your customer account.
  4. Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  5. Improve our website, products, and services.
  6. Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.
  7. Send promotional offers when you have given consent.
  8. Personalize your shopping experience.
  9. Analyze website performance and customer preferences.
  10. Enhance security and protect against unauthorized access.

How We Collect Your Information

  • Information you provide through contact and registration forms.
  • Details of purchases and transactions made on our website.
  • Email correspondence and customer service interactions.
  • Cookies and website analytics data.
  • Feedback, reviews, and survey responses.

Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send newsletters, promotions, and updates about new coffee products, special offers, and events. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal information.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Request a copy of your stored information.

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may only be shared with trusted service providers when necessary to deliver our services or comply with legal requirements.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team through the Contact page.
